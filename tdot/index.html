Here is a collection of video-feedback loops generated within CindyJS

In order to run these examples you require a webcam that points to your screen. You might use an external screen and your internal webcam, an USB-connected camera or you can use your smartphone as webcam together with an app like DroidCam.

In those examples you can press C to toggle the code.
